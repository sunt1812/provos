About Us

Pediatric Dentist in Provo, Utah

Our team is excited for the opportunity to welcome you and your child into our dental family at Provo Pediatric Dentistry. We offer comprehensive care for children to help them achieve smiles that remain healthy and strong for a lifetime. Our pediatric dentists, Dr. Lund, Dr. Markham and Dr. Bott, will work closely with our team to help walk you through every step of your child’s smile adventure!
